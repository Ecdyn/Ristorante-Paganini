Our Story

Welcome to Ristorante Paganini — your piece of Italy in the heart of Leipzig. For over 30 years we have run our restaurant with the same passion our family brought from our Italian homeland. We combine traditional recipes with fresh, carefully selected ingredients for an authentic taste experience.

Our cuisine lives by the simplicity and honesty of the Italian tradition. From handmade pasta to oven-fresh pizza — every dish tells a story of quality and dedication. We source our ingredients, wherever possible, directly from selected suppliers in Italy and the region.

Whether a relaxed lunch, a romantic dinner or an evening with friends — you are warmly welcome. Come in, take a seat and let our hospitality and cuisine enchant you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a reservation?
We recommend a reservation, especially on weekends and public holidays. During the week, tables are often available at short notice. Reserve conveniently by phone at +49 341 XXXXXXXX or use our online reservation system.
Do you have a business lunch?
Yes, we offer a changing business lunch menu Monday to Friday from 11:30 to 14:30. Our lunch menu includes a starter, pasta, main course and dessert — freshly prepared with seasonal ingredients at an attractive price.
Where can I park?
Parking is available close by on Große Fleischergasse and in the Brühl Center car park, just 200 metres away. Further public parking is available throughout Leipzig city centre.
Do you have vegetarian or vegan dishes?
Of course. Our menu offers a selection of vegetarian dishes including pasta, pizza and antipasti. For vegan guests we are happy to adapt our dishes — just ask us.
Do you have a terrace?
Yes, our restaurant has a welcoming terrace right in the heart of Leipzig city centre. In fine weather you can enjoy your meal outdoors — the terrace is open seasonally from spring to autumn.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash and all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, EC card). Contactless payment is also available.
Are dogs welcome?
Yes, well-behaved dogs are very welcome — both inside and on our terrace. We are happy to provide a water bowl.
